Download the test questions from the Blackboard Quiz Generator

Click "Download Question Pool" button and save the zip file to a location on your computer hard drive (eg. downloads folder or Desktop).

Navigate to the Pool section in blackboard
  1. In Blackboard, Click on "Control Panel" - "Course Tools
  2. Click "Test, Surveys and Pools"
  3. Click on "Pools"
Select Import Pool
  1. Click on "Import Pool" button
Select Browse
  1. Click on the "Browse My Computer" button
Select the exported file on your computer
  1. Click on the chevron to view question types as well as sample questions.
  2. Click on "Open"
Click Submit
  1. Click on "Submit" button to add questions to the pool manager.
